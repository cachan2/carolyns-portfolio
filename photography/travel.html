Some of my favorite photos when I was traveling are the fruit and pineapple stands and the palm trees in hawaii. I was experimenting with taking photos around the island, and I really enjoyed the vibrant colors of the food stand and the classic palm trees that we find in hawaii.

Something about the local food stand that my family found during a long drive felt very exciting and serene since it was in the middle of nowhere. The palm trees were taken at turtle cove, where I was trying to take palm tree silhouettes with the sunset.
