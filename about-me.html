Hello

My name is Carolyn Chan, and I am a third year design and communication double major. I went into design because I wanted to be able to work in a place where I can help people using my skills as an artist. When I found design, I found that I could apply what I know to create functional messages and narratives for users. Not only do I enjoy design, I enjoy taking photos everywhere I go, and experimenting in different traditional art mediums during my free times.
